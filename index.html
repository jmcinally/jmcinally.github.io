About Me

I specialize in building the "brains" of global organizations. With over 8 years of experience at Spotify and Multiverse, I architect systems that turn fragmented information into scalable, AI-ready knowledge. From launching international support hubs in Manila, Sofia and Montego Bay, to engineering custom AI content pipelines that save thousands of operational hours, my focus is always on the intersection of technical infrastructure and human performance. I don't just build documentation; I build ecosystems that empower teams to do their best work.

When I'm not "industrializing" documentation pipelines or leading global system rollouts, you'll find me in my home lab. My house is a living network of PCs, Raspberry Pis and ESP32s running Home Assistant and all manner of Docker containers. I bring that same "maker" curiosity to my professional work, connecting the dots between complex engineering logic and user-centric clarity.
